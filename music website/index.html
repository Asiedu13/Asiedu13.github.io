MUSIC

Music is the great uniter. An incredible force. Something that people who differ on everything and anything else can have in common.” ― Sarah Dessen

Music, like other objects has been defined in various ways. One of such definitions is the combination of sounds either natural or artificial and rhythms in an organized form over a period of time such that it pleases the ear.
OR
It is a pattern of sound that is produced by natural things such as the chirping of a bird and human being singing or artificial as in the case of a musical instrument.

Elements Of Music

Air is made up of different gases. It is basically the substance formed from a mixture of some gases such as carbon dioxide, oxygen ,hydrogen ,etc. These gases combined give us air and are what are known in music as the elements of music.
Elements of music then can be defined as different items that come together to form music or to make sounds well organized and complete to anyone listening.

Families of musical instruments

There are a variety of musical instruments in different countries all over the world. These instruments based on how they are played or their characteristics are grouped into categories.

Type Examples

Idiophones Castanet, Cymbal, Xylophone
